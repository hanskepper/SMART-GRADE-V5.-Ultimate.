1. Information We Collect

SMART GRADE collects only the information necessary for the application to function properly. All data is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly use cloud backup features.

We collect the following information:

Student Information

Name, class, student number, and PIN

Academic Data

Grades, subjects, coefficients, goals

Achievements

Badges, streaks, and progress history

Biometric Data

Fingerprint hash (stored locally only)

Profile Information

Profile photo (Base64 encoded), biography

User Content

Flashcards, notes, custom data

Important: We do NOT collect email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, or any form of contact information unless voluntarily provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating academic averages and statistics
  • Tracking achievements and streaks
  • Generating performance reports and charts
  • Facilitating data transfer between devices
  • Creating backups (local or cloud)
  • Authenticating your identity (PIN and fingerprint)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage Location

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using the browser's localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use cloud features
  • You have full control over your data
  • No external servers have access to your information

Optional Cloud Features:

  • Cloud Backup: When you use this feature, your encrypted data is stored on JSONBin.io or GitHub Gist servers.
  • Data Transfer: Temporary storage on GitHub Gist (expires after 10 minutes).
Your Control: You can export, delete, or backup your data at any time from the Settings page.

4. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your information:

  • PIN Protection: Your account is protected by a 4-digit PIN
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional fingerprint login (stored locally)
  • Local Storage: Data never leaves your device by default
  • Automatic Cleanup: Expired transfer codes are automatically deleted
  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete all your data

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We recommend:

  • Keeping your PIN confidential
  • Exporting important backups regularly
  • Using a strong PIN (not sequential numbers like 1234)

5. Your Rights

As a user of SMART GRADE, you have the following rights regarding your data:

Right to Access

View all your stored data at any time

Right to Export

Download your data in JSON, CSV, HTML, or PDF format

Right to Deletion

Delete your account and all associated data

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate information

Right to Restrict Processing

Stop using cloud features at any time

Right to Data Portability

Transfer your data to another device

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an account. Specifically:

  • Active Account: All data is kept until you delete your account
  • Transfer Codes: Automatically deleted after 10 minutes
  • Cloud Backups: Deleted after 24 hours or when you delete them
  • Deleted Account: All data is permanently removed from your device
Recommendation: Before deleting your account, use the Export feature to save a backup of your data.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

SMART GRADE does not use traditional cookies. Instead, we use the browser's localStorage API to store your data:

  • What is localStorage? A web storage mechanism that allows websites to store data locally on your device.
  • Is localStorage a cookie? No, localStorage is different from cookies and cannot be accessed by third parties.
  • Can I clear it? Yes, you can clear localStorage through your browser settings or by deleting your account.
  • How long does it stay? Until you manually delete it or clear your browser data.

No tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies are used in SMART GRADE.

8. Third-Party Services

SMART GRADE uses the following third-party services (all optional):

GitHub Gist

Used for cloud transfer (optional). Your data is stored temporarily (10 minutes).

Chart.js

Used for displaying statistics charts. No data is sent to Chart.js servers.

QR Code Libraries

Used for generating and scanning QR codes. All processing is local.

Google Fonts

Used for displaying custom fonts. May collect IP addresses.

Font Awesome

Used for icons. No personal data is collected.

These services are used only when you explicitly activate the corresponding features (cloud transfer, etc.).
